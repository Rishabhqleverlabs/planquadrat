How it all started

Herbert Elfers and Jörg Krämer found a students' working group "Südbahnhof“ in Darmstadt.

Martin Geskes, who had already managed his own architects' office in Frankfurt from 1986 until 1992, gets to know his later partners whilst doing his dissertation at the TU Darmstadt.

The three young and qualified architects, Herbert Elfers, Martin Geskes and Jörg Krämer join up to form their first working group.
